# 🌍 OrbitalGuard AI
### Space Traffic Management & Situational Awareness Platform

OrbitalGuard AI is an advanced Space Traffic Management and Space Situational Awareness platform designed to monitor satellites, track orbital debris, predict collision risks, and provide intelligent maneuver recommendations through an immersive 3D digital twin of Earth's orbital environment.

The platform aims to assist future satellite operators, researchers, and space agencies in understanding and mitigating the growing challenges of space congestion and orbital debris.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🌍 Realistic 3D Earth Visualization
- Ultra-realistic 3D Earth rendering
- Dynamic atmosphere glow
- Day/Night cycle
- Cloud layer animation
- HDR space environment
- Deep-space starfield background

### 🛰️ Satellite Tracking
- Real-time satellite visualization
- Multiple orbital layers:
  - LEO (Low Earth Orbit)
  - MEO (Medium Earth Orbit)
  - GEO (Geostationary Orbit)
  - Polar Orbit
  - Sun-Synchronous Orbit
- Satellite search and selection
- Interactive satellite tracking mode

### ☄️ Space Debris Monitoring
- Orbital debris visualization
- Debris density analysis
- Threat-level classification
- Collision threat indicators

### 🚨 Collision Detection Engine
- Closest Point of Approach (CPA) calculation
- Collision probability estimation
- High-risk encounter alerts
- Relative velocity computation

### 🤖 AI-Powered Risk Analysis
- AI-based collision risk assessment
- Intelligent threat analysis
- Autonomous maneuver recommendations
- Fuel optimization suggestions

### 📈 Future Trajectory Prediction
- Satellite orbit forecasting
- Future path simulation
- Predicted encounter visualization
- Time-based orbital simulation

### ⏱️ Digital Twin Simulation
- Real-time orbital simulation
- Timeline controls
- Future orbit playback
- Space traffic visualization

### ☀️ Space Weather Monitoring
- Solar activity monitoring
- Space weather analytics
- Geomagnetic disturbance analysis
